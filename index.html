LuBan Sequence Assembler

LuBan provides a Modular Cut option that allows you to #MakeItBig. However, assembly can become overwhelming if a large number of pieces are provided. Additionally, assembling in the wrong order can prevent some pieces from fitting together. Fortunately, LuBan creates a Sequence.txt file that lays out the cut-order... UNFORTUNATELY, this is a multi-dimensional array of numbers that don't seem to have rhyme nor reason to the untrained eye.

NOW, simply copy/paste your Sequence.txt, decide whether you want to deal with the Tiny pieces, click 'GO', and your Assembly instructions will be laid out in plain english. (Sorry folks, that's my native language)

(Default Sequence.txt provided is the Cow example shown in LuBan's Documentation)
